Dr's Clinic Privacy Policy

Welcome to Dr's Clinic, your trusted online healthcare provider. At Dr's Clinic, we are committed to maintaining the privacy and security of your personal information. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your data. By using our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

When you use our services, we may collect various types of information, including but not limited to:

  • Personal Information: Your name, contact information, date of birth, and medical history.
  • Health Information: Information about your health conditions, prescriptions, treatments, and interactions with healthcare providers.
  • Usage Information: Data about how you interact with our website and mobile app, including device information, IP address, and browser type.
  • Cookies: We may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and collect data for analytics.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Providing Healthcare Services: Your personal and health information is used to provide medical consultations, diagnosis, and treatment plans.
  • Improving Services: We analyze data to enhance our website, app, and services for a better user experience.
  • Communication: We may contact you for appointment reminders, health updates, or to respond to your inquiries.
  • Legal Requirements: We may share information when required by law or in response to legal requests.

Disclosure of Information

We may share your information with third parties for the following purposes:

  • Healthcare Providers: Your information is shared with our network of licensed healthcare professionals to provide medical services.
  • Service Providers: We may engage third-party service providers to assist us in delivering our services, such as hosting and payment processing.
  • Legal Obligations: We will disclose information if required to comply with applicable laws or respond to legal requests.
  • Consent: We may share your information with your explicit consent for specific purposes.

Data Security

We take data security seriously and employ industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: You have the right to correct any inaccuracies in your data.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your data under certain circumstances.
  • Objection: You may object to the processing of your data for certain purposes.
